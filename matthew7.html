Observation & Reflection

(Matthew 7 focus)

1. What contrasts does Jesus use in this chapter?
Wide and narrow gates, healthy and diseased trees, wise and foolish builders.

2. How does Jesus describe genuine discipleship?
Look for phrases about doing his words, not just hearing them.
